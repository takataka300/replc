Lost or Damaged KCSE Certificate – Requirements & Assistance

Requirements for KCSE Certificate Replacement

  • Original or copy of KCSE result slip (if available)
  • National ID or Birth Certificate copy
  • Police Abstract for lost certificate cases
  • Sworn affidavit explaining the loss or damage
  • Gazette notice (where applicable)
  • Passport size photographs
  • KNEC replacement application form
  • Replacement processing fee as required by KNEC
